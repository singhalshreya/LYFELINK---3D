ABOUT

LYFELINK is a rental system website that uses AR to offer a virtual simulation of the Property. It allows you to rent or lease access to 3D models for a set period of time. These models are often used in various industries, such as architecture, engineering. The website may allow users to browse a library of available 3D models, select the models they want to rent, and pay for accessing them. It makes visiting various apartments and flats less time-consuming as users can easily view the 3D model of the apartment, it also makes it convenient for customers to operate the whole thing without time wastage.
